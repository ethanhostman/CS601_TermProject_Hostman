I am an avid movie theater lover.

I have a movie pass to my favorite local theater. My friends and I make an effort to see a movie at least once a month. Although, I am guilty of always checking reviews first from both IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes. I love getting my hands nice and buttery with some popcorn (despite it always being overpriced). I had to drive over an hour to get to the theater growing up, so I love being five minutes away from a theater now.

Nope

My favorite movie of the Summer was definitely Nope! It was a great horror movie from director Jordan Peele. There was a genuinely spooky atmosphere mixed with some great fantasy adventure reminiscent of classic Spielberg movies. I loved seeing Keke Palmer on the big screen. Her energy and style were iconic.
