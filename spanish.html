A fun fact about me is that I'm fluent in Spanish!

Chihuahua, Mexico

When I was 19, I moved to Mexico for a couple of years to learn Spanish. It was an incredible experience and one that I will never forget. I lived in the state of Chihuahua. I loved immersing myself in the culture, meeting new people, and enjoying the delicious food.

When I returned to the United States, I attended college and majored in Spanish! I enjoyed learning about Spanish in an academic setting and even considered becoming a Spanish professor. However, I ultimately decided to pursue a career in software development. I hope to eventually specialize in natural language processing and artificial intelligence, aiming to improve our abilities to connect with other cultures via machine translation and similar softwares.
